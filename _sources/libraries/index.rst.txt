
Libraries
-----------

Currently included in scikit-tda are the libraries 

- UMAP, 
- Kepler Mapper, 
- Ripser and 
- Persim. 

These libraries provide facilities for

- Dimensionality Reduction
- Mapper
- Persistent Homology
- Persistence Images


What other aspects of TDA should be included?

- Distances on diagrams
- Statistics on diagrams
- Euler characteristic curves?
- Support of other types of PH. Zigzig?
